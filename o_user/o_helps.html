FAQs

  • How do I create an account on MavPark?

    To create an account on MavPark, simply click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the homepage. Fill in the required information and click "Create Account".

  • What payment methods do you accept?

    We accept all major credit cards, including Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover.

  • Can I cancel or modify my parking reservation?

    Yes, you can cancel or modify your parking reservation up to 24 hours before your scheduled arrival time. Simply log in to your account and make the changes from there.

  • How do I find my reserved parking spot?

    Once you arrive at the parking facility, simply follow the signs to your reserved parking spot. You can also refer to your confirmation email for specific instructions.

  • What if I arrive early or late for my parking reservation?

    If you arrive earlier or later than your scheduled arrival time, your parking reservation will still be honored. However, if you arrive earlier, you may have to wait until your reserved time slot becomes available.
